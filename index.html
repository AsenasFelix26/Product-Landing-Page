Premium Materials

One of Vans' shoes signature features is their vulcanised rubber construction, which is praised by skaters for its superior grip and support while on the board. Vulcanised rubber is unique in that it goes through two stages of ‘baking’: rubber is cured by heat and compressed, then a second ‘steam baking’ takes place after assembly to ensure a durable yet easy-to-break-in shoe.
